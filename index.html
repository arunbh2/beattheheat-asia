Ahmedabad Cool Roofs Initiative

Mahila Housing Trust + AMC

Solar-reflective coating reducing indoor temperatures by 2-5°C. Successfully deployed in 15,000+ slum households.

Impact: 2-5°C temperature reduction | 75,000 beneficiaries
Funding Needed: ₹5 Cr for 10,000 more homes

Extreme Heat Income Insurance

SEWA + Arsht-Rock

Parametric insurance providing automatic payouts to informal workers when extreme heat conditions occur.

Impact: 50,000 women workers covered | ₹500 average payout
Scale-up: ₹10 Cr to expand to 5 new cities
🌳

Urban Forest Initiative - Pune

PMC + Terracon Foundation

Creating micro-forests using Miyawaki method, reducing local temperatures by 3-4°C and improving air quality.

Progress: 23 forests | 1.2L trees | 45 hectares
Expansion: ₹3 Cr for 20 new sites
